About Us

The Institute for Economic and Strategic Affairs is a leading independent think tank dedicated to producing high-quality research that informs policymakers, experts, and the public. Our work spans global economic policy, strategic security analysis, technology innovation, and sustainable development to tackle the challenges of the 21st century.

Research Areas

Global Economic Policy

Deep analysis of international trade, monetary systems, and development trends that shape the global economy.

Security and Defence Strategy

Assessment of emerging security threats, defense policies, and geopolitical risks to inform national and international strategies.

Technology & Innovation

Exploring how innovation and disruptive technologies influence economic growth and alter strategic balances worldwide.

Climate Change and Sustainability

Providing frameworks and policy recommendations to advance sustainability and mitigate climate risks globally.
